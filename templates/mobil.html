Mobil Shortcut

To make it easier to listen to KOFG on the go download a link to go directly to the player. The process could vary slightly depending on your phone mode. If you have any trouble, try searching “how to download link to home screen (your phone model)” should bring up a guide specific to your phone.
‍

Safari Instructions

‍Step 1 Go to KOFG
Launch Safari and open www.kofgfm.com
‍
Step 2 Open Browser Menu
Slide for finger down, and your iphone menu will open.
‍
Step 3 Click Share
Select the "Share" icon. You can also access this share button to the left of the URL by tapping the top of the page.

Step 4 Save to Home
Select "save to home screen" option.
‍
Congratulations! Click this short cut link anytime you want to stream KOFG Radio.

Chrom Instructions

‍Step 1 Go to KOFG
Launch Chrome and search www.kofgfm.com
‍
Step 2 Open Browser Menu
Tap the screen to make the menu button appear and select "Add to home screen".
It looks like three tots on the right hand corner.
‍
Yay, you did it! Click this short cut link anytime you want to stream KOFG Radio.
