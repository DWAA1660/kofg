Pear of Grace Ranch

Pearl of Grace Ranch is a non-profit residential ministry dedicated to helping troubled teen girls in Oklahoma and surrounding areas. After a two year renovation process of our facility, our doors are now open! We provide a secure environment where the girls are loved no matter what baggage they bring from their past.
